Privacy

This policy covers how RobotLovesKitty treats personal information that RobotLovesKitty collects and receives, including information related to your past use of RobotLovesKitty products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available.


Information Collection and Use

RobotLovesKitty collects personal information when you register with RobotLovesKitty, when you use RobotLovesKitty products or services, and when you visit RobotLovesKitty pages or the pages of certain RobotLovesKitty partners. RobotLovesKitty may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies.

When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, zip code etc. Once you register with RobotLovesKitty and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.

RobotLovesKitty collects information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners.

RobotLovesKitty automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, RobotLovesKitty cookie information, and the page you request.

RobotLovesKitty uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

Children

RobotLovesKitty does not presently accept registrations from children under age 13.


Information Sharing and Disclosure

RobotLovesKitty does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

  • We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with RobotLovesKitty under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help RobotLovesKitty communicate with you about offers from RobotLovesKitty and our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information.
  • We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims.
  • We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of RobotLovesKitty's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
  • We transfer information about you if RobotLovesKitty is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, RobotLovesKitty will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
Cookies

RobotLovesKitty may set and access RobotLovesKitty cookies on your computer.


Communications from RobotLovesKitty

Certain communications from RobotLovesKitty, such as service announcements and, administrative messages are considered part of RobotLovesKitty membership and you may not opt out of receiving them. In particular those who register with us for beta testing RobotLovesKitty' games agree to receive communications regarding these games as a part of their beta membership.

All other emails from RobotLovesKitty will be strictly 'opt-in' communications, and will include a URL or email address where you can unsubscribe from future mailings.

You can delete your RobotLovesKitty account by visiting our Account Deletion page.


Confidentiality and Security

Your RobotLovesKitty Account Information is password-protected.

In certain areas RobotLovesKitty uses industry-standard SSLencryption to protect data transmissions.

We take security seriously and make our best efforts to prevent compromise of your personal information. In the event of a serious security breach, we will alert our registered users.


Changes to this Privacy Policy

RobotLovesKitty may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the email address specified in your RobotLovesKitty account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
