The Beginning


It took us 5 years to make, and was an excellent education into programing, art, and game design. Sadly, Neverdaunt was unfinished. There were a few imperfections in the core gameplay, unresolved bugs, and despite our best attempts at optimization, slow loading times. Because of this, we never properly marketed the game and eventually our server costs outweighed our income. We took the servers offline.
